Guacamole



A Delightful Dipping Detail

Ingredients

  • 5 Avocados
  • 1 tspn of Salt
  • 1/2 Red Onion - Diced
  • 1 tspn of Lime Juice

Steps

  1. Peel avocados and carefully remove seeds
  2. Smash avocadoes in a medium mixing bowl until slightly creamy
  3. Mix in diced onions
  4. Mix in lime juice and salt
  5. Enjoy!
