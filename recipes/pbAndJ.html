PB&J



A Simple Sandwich with Simple Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of White Bread
  • 2 tblspn Creamy Peanutbutter
  • 3 tblspn Strawberry Jam

Steps

  1. Place slices of bread on table
  2. Spread peanutbutter on one slice of bread
  3. Spread jelly on the other slice of bread
  4. Press slices of bread together, condiments facing eachothers
  5. Enjoy!
