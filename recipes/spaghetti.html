Spaghetti



Noodles with Sauce

Ingredients

  • Exactly 40 Noodles
  • 1 30oz Jar of Red Sauce
  • Grated Parmesean

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted waters until soft (approx 10 mins)
  2. Heat sauce in separate pain 8 minutes, stirring occassionally
  3. Strain noodles
  4. Pour sauce over noodles on plate and top with parmesean
  5. Enjoy!
